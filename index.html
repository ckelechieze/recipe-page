Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette is a great way to start your day with a delicious and nutritious breakfast.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Optional fillings: cheese, ham, vegetables
  • 1 tablespoon of butter oil

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs: into a bowl, add salt, and beat until well combined.
  2. Heat the butter: in a non-stick skillet over medium heat until melted and slightly bubbling.
  3. Pour the beaten eggs: into the skillet, tilting it to spread the eggs evenly.
  4. Cook for 1-2 minutes: until the edges start to set. You can lift the edges with a spatula to allow uncooked egg to flow underneath.
  5. Add your desired fillings: on one half of the omelette.
  6. Fold the omelette: Once the omelette is mostly set but still slightly runny on top, fold it in half over the fillings.
  7. Cook for another minute: until the cheese is melted and the omelette is cooked through.
  8. Slide the omelette onto a plate: and serve immediately. Enjoy!
